隐私政策

ZH-CN
一、适用范围

本隐私政策适用于由 ScrollShot（以下简称“本应用”）提供的服务。我们非常重视您的隐私和数据安全，并承诺尽量减少对您个人信息的收集和使用。

二、我们收集哪些信息

在您使用本应用的过程中，我们不会收集、存储或上传可识别您个人身份的信息，包括但不限于：姓名、邮箱、电话号码、通讯录、位置信息等。

本应用仅在本地处理以下数据，用于实现核心功能：

  • 屏幕内容 / 截图内容：仅用于生成长截图、拼接图片等功能；仅在您的设备本地进行处理，不会被上传至任何服务器。
  • 应用必要日志（如有）：仅用于在本地排查错误或优化体验；不包含可识别您个人身份的信息；不会上传到服务器。
三、权限使用说明

本应用可能会请求以下系统权限，仅用于实现相应功能，不会用于与功能无关的目的：

  • 存储 / 媒体访问权限（如有）：用于保存生成的长截图、读取您选择的图片进行拼接；仅访问您选择的文件或本应用生成的文件。
  • 屏幕捕获 / 录屏相关权限（如有）：用于捕获当前屏幕内容以生成长截图或滚动截图；仅在您主动发起操作时捕获当前屏幕画面；仅在本地用于合成图片，不会上传。

如后续版本增加新的权限或用途，我们会在更新版本中明确告知您。

四、数据的存储与传输
  • 本应用 不自建服务器，不会将您的数据上传到任何远程服务器。
  • 截图、拼接结果等数据均 保存在您的设备本地存储中，您可以随时删除。
  • 除非您通过第三方应用（如社交平台、网盘等）主动分享图片，否则本应用不会将数据传输给任何第三方。
五、第三方服务

当前版本本应用不集成任何第三方广告、统计或分析 SDK。

如未来版本接入第三方服务（如广告平台、统计分析工具等），我们将更新本隐私政策，并在应用内或应用商店页面中额外说明第三方服务的名称、类型及其数据处理方式。

六、儿童隐私

本应用面向普通用户，未专门以 13 岁以下儿童为目标用户。

我们不会主动收集任何儿童的个人信息。

如您认为我们在未取得监护人同意的情况下收集了儿童信息，请通过下方联系方式与我们取得联系，我们会尽快删除相关数据（如有）。

七、您的权利

由于本应用不收集可识别个人身份的信息，一般情况下：

  • 无需进行账号注册或登录；
  • 无需提交删除账号或导出数据的请求。

您可以在系统设置中卸载本应用，以删除本应用在本机存储的全部数据（截图文件除外，如需一并删除，请在文件管理中手动删除相关图片）。

八、本隐私政策的更新

我们可能会在必要时更新本隐私政策。

当政策发生实质性变更时，我们会通过应用更新说明或应用商店页面进行提示。

继续使用本应用即表示您同意受更新后的隐私政策约束。

Privacy Policy

EN
1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application ScrollShot (hereinafter referred to as “the App”). We value your privacy and are committed to minimizing the collection and use of your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we do not collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location.

The App only processes the following data locally on your device in order to provide its core functionalities:

  • Screen content / screenshots: used solely to capture and generate long screenshots or stitched images; processing is performed locally on your device only and is not uploaded to any server.
  • Basic app logs (if any): used only to diagnose errors and improve user experience; they do not contain personally identifiable information and are not uploaded to any server.
3. Use of Permissions

The App may request certain system permissions solely for functional purposes:

  • Storage / Media access (if used): to save generated long screenshots and to read images you select for stitching; access is limited to files you select or files generated by the App.
  • Screen capture / screen recording (if used): to capture the current screen for creating long or scrolling screenshots; only the screen content at the moment you initiate the action is captured, and it is used locally to generate images and not uploaded.

If we introduce any new permissions or purposes in future versions, we will clearly disclose them in the update.

4. Data Storage and Transfer
  • The App does not host its own servers and does not upload your data to any remote server.
  • Screenshots and stitched images are stored locally on your device, and you may delete them at any time.
  • Unless you choose to share images via third-party apps (such as social networks or cloud storage), the App will not transmit your data to any third party.
5. Third-Party Services

In the current version, the App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

If we integrate third-party services in the future (e.g., ad networks, analytics tools), we will update this Privacy Policy and clearly disclose the names, types, and data practices of such services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us using the information below, and we will promptly delete such data (if any).

7. Your Rights and Choices

Because the App does not collect personally identifiable information:

  • There is no need to create or log into an account.
  • There is generally no need to request data export or deletion.

You may uninstall the App from your device at any time to remove all App-related data stored by the App (excluding screenshots saved in your gallery; you may delete those manually if needed).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When material changes are made, we will notify you via the app update notes or the app store listing.

Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions, comments, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or data protection, please contact us at:
